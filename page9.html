Project Limited Edition Beatles Turntable

John Doe 19/2/17

With four more Beatles turntables on the way this turntable is based on the Debut Carbon Esprit SB

Working alongside Universal Music Gruop Pro-Ject have announced a total of five special edition turntables

The 1964 Turntable, yes that is what this turntables is called, includes copies of original tickets for the bands concerts between 1962-1966.

The design is done by a process of image transfer. The tickets appear in black ink on a white turntable. The ink is dried out using special ultra-violet lights which ensure details arent lost or smeared.

The turntable components are that of the five star Debut Carbon Espirit SB, which uses an Ortofon 2M Red stylus which is attached to a carbon tone-arm. The turntable features an acrylic platter as well as built in electronic Speedcontrol for switching speeds

If it’s musical fluency and ease of speed change you are after this Pro-Ject will have a lot of appeal, especially considering the ease of cable upgrade.

With only 2500 of these turntables available worldwide, enthusiastic Beatles fans will have to snap these up early
