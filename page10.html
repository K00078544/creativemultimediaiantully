Ballfinger's New Reel-to-Reel Tape Machine

Mark Ryan 16/2/17

Combining retro tech with a modern design aesthetic

This Reel-to-Reel is Made by German style brand Ballfinger. Ballfinger's background in stylish furnishings and accessories producing products such as wristwatches and anglepoise lamps aimed at professionals.

The Tonbandmaschine M063 and its components are all manufactured in Ballfinger factories.

The M063 comes with a three-motor drive that runs at 7.5 or 15in per second and can take up to 12in tape reels. Thanks to tacho-controlled winding speeds and processor-controlled drive the reel-to-reel runs almost silently by keeping tape taut, preventing stretching or forming of loops. There is a digital counter built in for precise readouts.

Tape machine and matching record deck were unveiled at German hi-fi show Norddeutsche Hifi tage

As I said above this product is aimed at professionals and that can be seen in its pricing of €27,000
