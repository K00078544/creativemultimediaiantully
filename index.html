Programming and Design for Beginners

Ian Tully 30-3-2017

Exploring the word of design on the go

Creative Multimedia And Programming offers rewarding and challenging possibilities for learners interested in entering the world of multimedia. To see websites created by you online can be a rewarding feeling, to see others are viewing your site is even better.

The Creative Multimedia and Progamming cousrse at LIT (Limerick Institute of Technology) offers students the opportunity to develop skills in creative programs such as Adobe Illustrstor, Photoshop, Premiere Pro, 3DS Max Autodesk and using programming techniques ranging from PHP, MySql, Javascript and XML.

Given the size of the IT sector in Ireland and the current growth of roles associated with the IT sector, there is a clear demand for professionals with computing and IT skills. Not only in Ireland but also worldwide.

Projected IT sector Growth

Employment in computer design and related servies are expected to grow rapidly over the next decade, pacing ahead of other similar professional, technical and scientific industries. It is expected that between 2010 and 2020 output in computer systems design and web development is expected to grow at an average annual rate of 6.1 percent, compared to the 3.6 percent of science and technology sectors and the 2.9 percent of all other industries.
